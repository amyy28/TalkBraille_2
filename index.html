We are helping towards the ones
who cannot see but have a clear vision.


Three prodcuts made exclusively for bettering the education system of the blind.

Current Scenario

Braille Text and image printed in separate pages using normal affordable printers. Thermal printers which can do the job cost Rs 70 per page

Our Solution

Soft Braille, which can print both images and text on same page using normal printers at a price of not more than Rs 5

Our Products

Soft Braille, Auducator and TALK for enhancing the education and understanding of blind students with easy impletation of computer technology

Our Solution

We have built 3 exclusive products which we feel can solve their problem and make their
study really interesting.
