Tactile Auditory Learning Kit

In many educational institutes, subjects requiring pictorial representations such as graph-theory, Geography, Environmental Studies etc are often omitted due to the large number of special educators required to support the students to teach such topics using tactiles. Pictures and figures represented as tactiles on paper are very hard to comprehend for the Visually impaired without the assistance of special educators over multiple sessions of teaching. TALK enables the visually impaired students to use their combination of touch with aural feedback from our software to better comprehend these tactiles. The use of this software helps drastically cut down the number of teachers required beyond a certain point.


The special educator will be required to create the content by assigning labels to regions in the figure. This will have to be done only once for any set of tactiles. Once the content is created the visually impaired can use it through our software. The TALK software track the fingers of the visually impaired student over the tactile page. Whenever the person places his finger over a labelled region, the software will read out the label header to the student.The student can associate the regions on the tactile with the aural feedback he hears. This enables him to memorise the image.
